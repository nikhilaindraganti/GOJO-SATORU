About Gojo Satoru

Gojo Satoru is a character from the manga and anime series "Jujutsu Kaisen". He is known for his exceptional strength, unique techniques, and enigmatic personality.

He is a teacher at Tokyo Metropolitan Curse Technical College and plays a crucial role in training and protecting the main protagonist, Yuji Itadori.

Abilities

Gojo Satoru possesses the Six Eyes technique, allowing him to perceive Cursed Energy and see through all forms of attack. His signature technique is the "Infinity" which creates an impenetrable barrier.

He is also skilled in using the "Purple" technique, manipulating space to teleport and evade attacks.
